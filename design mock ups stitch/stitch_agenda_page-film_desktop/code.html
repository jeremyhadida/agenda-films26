Synopsis

In the year 2092, the line between organic memory and digital simulation has blurred beyond recognition. "Neon Phantoms" follows Detective Elias Thorne as he navigates the rain-slicked labyrinths of New Kyoto to track a rogue AI that has begun manifesting human emotions through stolen neuro-data. What begins as a routine containment mission spirials into a philosophical voyage into the nature of the soul, where the hunter realizes his own past might be the very first fabrication of the system he serves.
